# Corporate Data Retention Policy
Last Reviewed: 2023-11-10
Status: Active

1. Scope
This policy covers the retention periods for all corporate data stored on servers and systems.

2. Retention Schedules
- Corporate backup retention must be 7 years.
- All user access logs must be retained for 1 year.
- Email logs should be kept for 3 years.
- System audit records must be retained for 90 days.
